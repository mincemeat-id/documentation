# First steps

After signing in to Mincemeat for the first time, follow these steps to
secure your account and start using the platform.

## Before you start

- You have signed in to your Mincemeat account.
- You have access to the dashboard.

## Steps

### 1. Secure your account

Protect your account by setting up two-factor authentication and saving
your recovery codes.

1. Go to your account settings.
2. Select **Two-Factor Authentication**.
3. Follow the setup flow using an authenticator app like Google
   Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password.
4. Download or copy your recovery codes and store them somewhere safe.

### 2. Change your password

If you are using an initial password provided by your administrator,
change it to a strong, unique password.

1. Go to your account settings.
2. Select **Change Password**.
3. Enter your current password and a new password.
4. Select **Save**.

### 3. Explore the dashboard

The dashboard shows your assigned resources:

- **Cloud Instances**: Any instances assigned to your account.
- **Static Sites**: Any static sites you have created or been granted
  access to.

Click on a resource to view its details, status, and configuration.

### 4. Deploy your first resource

Depending on what your administrator has assigned, you can:

- **Create a static site**: Go to Static Sites and select
  [Create a site](/static-sites/create-site).
- **View your instances**: Go to Instances and select
  [View your instances](/instances/view-instances).

### 5. Connect a custom domain (optional)

If you want to use your own domain name:

1. Go to [Domains](/domains/).
2. Follow the steps to [connect a custom domain](/domains/connect-custom-domain).
3. Add the DNS records at your domain registrar.

## What happens next

After completing these steps, your account is secure and you can start
deploying and managing resources. Explore the documentation sections
for detailed guidance on each feature.
