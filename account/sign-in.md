# Sign in

Sign in to Mincemeat with the email address and password for your
account. If two-factor authentication is enabled, Mincemeat asks for a
second verification step before opening the app.

## Before you start

- You need an active Mincemeat account.
- You need your password.
- If 2FA is enabled, you need your authenticator app or one unused
  recovery code.

## Steps

1. Open the Mincemeat app.
2. Enter your email address in **Email**.
3. Enter your password in **Password**.
4. Select **Sign in**.
5. If Mincemeat shows **Two-Factor Authentication**, enter the
   6-digit code from your authenticator app or paste one unused
   recovery code.
6. Select **Verify**.

## What happens next

After a successful sign-in, Mincemeat opens the page you were trying to
visit or the dashboard. Your session can continue across page refreshes,
but you may be asked to sign in again after a period of inactivity or
after your account permissions change.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to check |
| --- | --- |
| Your password is not accepted | Check that you are using the email address for this account and that Caps Lock is off. |
| You see **Two-Factor Authentication** but cannot access your app | Use one unused recovery code, then regenerate recovery codes after you sign in. |
| The 2FA code is rejected | Wait for the next code in your authenticator app and confirm your device time is set automatically. |
| An error includes a reference value | Copy the reference and follow [Use a correlation ID](/troubleshooting/correlation-ids). |
