# Change your password

Change your password from Account Settings when you want to rotate a
credential or protect your account after a device or password manager
problem.

## Before you start

- You must be signed in.
- You need your current password.
- Your new password must be at least 12 characters.

## Steps

1. Open **Account Settings**.
2. Select the **Change Password** tab.
3. Enter your current password in **Current Password**.
4. Enter a new password in **New Password**.
5. Enter the same new password in **Confirm New Password**.
6. To end all other active sessions, select **Sign out from all other
   devices**.
7. Select **Change Password**.

## What happens next

Mincemeat confirms that the password changed. If you selected **Sign
out from all other devices**, every other browser session is revoked;
your current session stays active so you can continue working.

The password change is recorded in **My Activity**.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to check |
| --- | --- |
| The form says the password is too short | Use at least 12 characters. A longer passphrase is usually easier to remember. |
| The confirmation does not match | Re-enter **New Password** and **Confirm New Password**. |
| You cannot remember your current password | Contact support through your normal support channel. Do not share old or new passwords in the request. |
| You think another device is signed in | Change your password again and select **Sign out from all other devices**. |
