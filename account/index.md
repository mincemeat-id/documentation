# Account

Manage your Mincemeat account: sign in and out, change your password,
set up two-factor authentication, store recovery codes, and review your
personal audit history.

Most account settings live under **Account Settings** in the app. The
sign-in and sign-out controls are separate because they appear before
and after your authenticated session.

| Task | Start here |

## Security habits

- Keep your password unique to Mincemeat.
- Enable two-factor authentication when your account has access to
  production sites or cloud instances.
- Store recovery codes in a password manager or another private place.
- Sign out from shared devices as soon as you are done.
